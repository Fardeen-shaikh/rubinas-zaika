Hotel Style Baingan Masala

A tangy and flavorful hotel-style baingan masala made with roasted peanut paste ...

3-4 servings
15 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 250 grams baingan (eggplant), washed
  • 1 large onion
  • Fresh coriander
  • Curry leaves (kadi patta)
  • Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • Dried coconut, roasted
  • Tamarind (imli), soaked in water
  • Oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Mustard seeds (rai) - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin (jeera) - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Peanuts (mungfali), finely ground
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Hot water (for gravy)

Directions

  1. 1.Cut slits in the baingan and soak in salted water to prevent blackening.
  2. 2.Heat oil in a kadhai, add cumin and mustard seeds.
  3. 3.Add onion and ginger garlic paste, fry until soft.
  4. 4.Add remaining dry masalas and fry. Add tomatoes and cook covered for 2 minutes.
  5. 5.Add curry leaves, mix, then add the baingan pieces. Mix well and cook covered for 2-3 minutes.
  6. 6.Add tamarind pulp (to taste) and finely ground peanut paste.
  7. 7.Add hot water for gravy, chopped coriander, and garam masala powder.
  8. 8.Cook covered on low flame for 5 minutes.

Tips

  • *Soak cut baingan in salted water to prevent it from turning black
  • *Grind the peanuts very fine for the paste
  • *Add tamarind pulp according to your taste preference
