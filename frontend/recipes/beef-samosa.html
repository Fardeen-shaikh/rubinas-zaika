Beef Samosa

Iftar special crispy beef samosas filled with a spiced beef keema mixture with f...

10-12 servings
20 min + 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg beef keema (mince)
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Black pepper powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 1 tablespoon
  • 4 large onions, finely chopped
  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Green chillies, chopped
  • Samosa patti (pastry strips)
  • Maida slurry (for sealing)
  • Oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. 1.Heat oil, fry ginger garlic paste. Add keema and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  2. 2.Add all dry masalas, mix well, cover and cook for 5 minutes until keema is cooked and water dries up.
  3. 3.Add chopped onions, coriander, and green chillies. Mix and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. 4.Let the filling cool.
  5. 5.Fold samosa patti into a cone shape, fill with keema mixture.
  6. 6.Seal with maida slurry and fold to close.
  7. 7.Heat oil until hot, reduce to low flame, and deep fry samosas until golden and crispy.

Tips

  • *You can use chicken or mutton keema instead of beef
  • *Fry on low flame after oil is hot for even cooking and crispy exterior
  • *Adding raw onion and coriander at the end gives fresh flavor
