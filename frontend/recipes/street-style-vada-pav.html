Street Style Vada Pav

Authentic street style vada pav with spicy potato filling, crispy besan coating,...

18-20 servings
20 min + 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 fistful fresh coriander
  • 1 fistful fresh mint
  • 10-12 green chillies, ground into paste
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 tablespoon
  • Carom seeds (ajwain) - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin - 1 tablespoon
  • Mustard seeds (rai) - 1 tablespoon
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon oil (for tempering)
  • Besan (gram flour) - 2 bowls, sifted
  • 1 tablespoon cumin (for batter)
  • 1 tablespoon carom seeds (for batter)
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt (for batter)
  • Water - about 1 bowl (for batter)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1-2 whole garlic bulbs
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder (for chutney)
  • 4 green chillies (for fried mirchi)
  • Pav bread for serving

Directions

  1. 1.Boil 1 kg potatoes, peel immediately while hot, and mash thoroughly with a masher.
  2. 2.Prepare tempering: heat 1 teaspoon oil, add mustard seeds and fry. Then add cumin and carom seeds. Add turmeric and salt. Fry on low flame for a few seconds.
  3. 3.Add green chilli paste, mint, and finely chopped coriander to the tempering. Fry for a few seconds. Add 1 tablespoon ginger paste and mix.
  4. 4.Pour the tempering into the mashed potatoes and mix thoroughly. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
  5. 5.Shape the potato mixture into round vadas using your palms — makes about 18-20 vadas from 1 kg potatoes.
  6. 6.Prepare batter: sift 2 bowls besan into a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon cumin, 1 tablespoon carom seeds, 1/2 tablespoon baking soda, 1/2 tablespoon salt. Add water gradually to make a thick batter. Rest for 10 minutes.
  7. 7.For the chutney: save some besan batter in a separate bowl. Deep fry small portions of the batter in hot oil until crispy. Fry 1-2 bulbs of garlic until golden in the same oil.
  8. 8.Grind the fried besan, fried garlic, and 1 tablespoon red chilli powder together to make the chutney.
  9. 9.Dip each vada into the besan batter, coat well, and deep fry in hot oil. Fry 3-4 at a time, about 1-2 minutes per side until golden and crispy.
  10. 10.For fried chillies: fry 4 green chillies in oil and sprinkle salt on them.
  11. 11.Serve the vada in pav bread with the garlic chutney and fried chillies.

Tips

  • *Peel and mash potatoes immediately after boiling for best texture
  • *Sift the besan to ensure a smooth batter
  • *Do not add all the water at once — add gradually for a thick batter
  • *Let the batter rest for 10 minutes before frying
  • *Once oil is hot, reduce flame to low for even frying
