Arabian Bread Pudding

A quick and delicious Arabian bread pudding layered with custard, bread, fresh c...

8-10 servings
20 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 litre full cream milk
  • 10-12 bread slices (crusts removed)
  • Milkmaid (condensed milk) - 200 grams
  • Fresh cream - 250 ml
  • Custard powder (vanilla flavor) - 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Cold milk - 1 cup (for custard slurry)
  • Cashews and almonds, crushed
  • Rose petals (for garnish)

Directions

  1. 1.Mix fresh cream and milkmaid together in a bowl.
  2. 2.Remove bread crusts from all slices.
  3. 3.Boil 1 litre milk. Dissolve custard powder in 1 cup cold milk, add to boiling milk with sugar. Cook for 2 minutes until thick.
  4. 4.Let custard cool to room temperature.
  5. 5.In a rectangular dish, layer: custard, bread slices, custard, cream-milkmaid mixture, dry fruits.
  6. 6.Repeat layers: bread, custard, cream-milkmaid mixture, dry fruits.
  7. 7.Continue layering until all ingredients are used.
  8. 8.Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  9. 9.Serve chilled with extra custard, cream-milkmaid mixture, dry fruits, and rose petals.

Tips

  • *Use full cream milk for best results
  • *Always dissolve custard powder in cold milk to avoid lumps
  • *Serve chilled - it must be cold for the best taste
  • *You can add pistachio and chironji to the dry fruits
