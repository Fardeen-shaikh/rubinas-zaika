Sitaphal Rabdi

A creamy sitaphal (custard apple) rabdi made with thickened milk and fresh sitap...

6-8 servings
20 min + 50 min

Ingredients

  • 5 ripe sitaphal (custard apples)
  • 1 litre full fat milk
  • Cardamom powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Saffron (kesar) - a few strands
  • Pistachios, sliced (for garnish)

Directions

  1. 1.Scoop out the pulp from sitaphal using a spoon.
  2. 2.Pass the pulp through a sieve to separate seeds. Refrigerate the pulp.
  3. 3.Boil 1 litre full fat milk in a non-stick pan on high flame.
  4. 4.Add saffron strands for color and flavor, reduce flame to low.
  5. 5.Keep stirring and scraping the sides, cooking until milk reduces by 50% (about 40-45 minutes).
  6. 6.Add sugar and cardamom powder, mix and cook 5 more minutes on low flame.
  7. 7.Turn off flame and let the rabdi cool to room temperature.
  8. 8.Mix in the sitaphal pulp and sliced pistachios.
  9. 9.Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving. Serve chilled.

Tips

  • *Use full fat milk for the best rabdi
  • *Don't add sitaphal pulp while the rabdi is hot - wait for it to cool
  • *Refrigerate the pulp while making rabdi to prevent color change
  • *Keep stirring to prevent milk from sticking to the bottom
  • *Serve chilled for the best taste
