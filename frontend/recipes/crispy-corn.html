Crispy Corn

Chatpata crispy corn — sweet corn kernels coated in spiced cornflour batter and ...

4-5 servings
10 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 kg sweet corn
  • Cornflour for coating
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chilli powder
  • Black pepper powder
  • Chaat masala
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Butter (optional)
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chillies, chopped

Directions

  1. 1.Boil water in a pot. Add sweet corn kernels and boil for 5-7 minutes until slightly soft. Don't overcook.
  2. 2.Drain the water completely. Let the corn cool slightly.
  3. 3.Coat the boiled corn with cornflour, salt, and a pinch of red chilli powder. Mix well so each kernel is evenly coated.
  4. 4.Heat oil in a kadhai. Deep fry the coated corn in batches on medium-high flame until golden and crispy.
  5. 5.In a separate pan, add butter. Add curry leaves, chopped green chillies, and the fried corn. Toss with salt, red chilli powder, black pepper, and chaat masala.
  6. 6.Serve hot as a snack.

Tips

  • *Don't overcook the corn while boiling — 5-7 minutes is enough
  • *Drain well before coating with cornflour
  • *Fry in batches for even crispiness
  • *Toss with butter and spices right before serving
