Masala Chai

Perfect masala chai made with freshly crushed ginger, cardamom, and black pepper...

4 servings
5 min + 10 min

Ingredients

  • Water - 2 cups
  • Full cream milk - 3 cups
  • Ginger - a piece
  • Cardamom - a few
  • Black pepper - 4-5
  • Tea leaves - 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons

Directions

  1. 1.Coarsely crush ginger, cardamom (remove shells first), and black pepper together. Don't grate the ginger - crushing gives better flavour.
  2. 2.Boil 2 cups water. Add the crushed masala and let it boil.
  3. 3.Add 1 tablespoon tea leaves and let it brew well.
  4. 4.Add 3 cups full cream milk (warm it before adding). Let it cook for 5 minutes.
  5. 5.Add sugar at the very end - adding it last keeps the tea thick. Stir and bring to one final boil.
  6. 6.Strain and serve hot.

Tips

  • *Crush the ginger, don't grate it - gives much better flavour
  • *Add sugar at the very end to keep the tea thick
  • *Use full cream milk for the richest taste
  • *Remove cardamom shells before crushing
