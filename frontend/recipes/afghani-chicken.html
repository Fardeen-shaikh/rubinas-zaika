Afghani Chicken

Restaurant-style Afghani chicken with a rich creamy cashew-almond gravy and smok...

4-5 servings
1 hr + 30 min

Ingredients

  • 750g chicken, washed
  • 1 lemon juice + 1/2 teaspoon salt (for cleaning)
  • Fresh coriander - 1 bowl
  • 2 large onions, thickly sliced
  • 7-8 green chillies
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Coriander powder - 2 tablespoons
  • Garam masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • 250g fresh yogurt (not sour)
  • 250g fresh cream
  • 15-20 cashews + 15-20 almonds (soaked, ground to paste with milk)
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 3 tablespoons + butter
  • Charcoal for smoking (dhungar)

Directions

  1. 1.Clean chicken with lemon and salt. Make deep cuts on each piece for masala to penetrate.
  2. 2.Make green paste: grind coriander, onion, and green chillies. Make cashew-almond paste: grind soaked cashews and almonds with a little milk.
  3. 3.Marinate chicken with ginger garlic paste, coriander powder, garam masala, cumin powder, black pepper, cashew-almond paste, green paste, yogurt, fresh cream, salt, and oil. Mix thoroughly.
  4. 4.Refrigerate and marinate for at least 1 hour (2-3 hours is better).
  5. 5.Heat oil and butter in a kadhai on high flame. Add marinated chicken and fry for 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. 6.Remove fried chicken pieces. In the same kadhai, add remaining marinade/gravy with a little water if needed.
  7. 7.Cook the gravy for 5 minutes until oil separates. Add the fried chicken back and mix well.
  8. 8.Cover and cook for 5 minutes. For smoky flavour: place a burning charcoal in a small bowl in the centre, add a little ghee on it, and immediately cover for a few minutes.
  9. 9.Remove the charcoal bowl. Garnish with coriander and serve.

Tips

  • *Make deep cuts in chicken for masala to penetrate
  • *Yogurt should not be sour for this recipe
  • *Marinate for at least 1 hour, longer is better
  • *The dhungar (smoking) step adds amazing smoky aroma
