Sitaphal Cream (Custard Apple Cream)

A quick and delicious no-cook dessert made with fresh custard apple (sitaphal) p...

6-8 servings
20 min + 0 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg cream (malai) — packaged or homemade
  • 190 ml condensed milk (Milkmaid)
  • 2 kg custard apple (sitaphal), ripe
  • Dry fruits for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. 1.Add 1/2 kg cream (malai) to a large bowl.
  2. 2.Add 190 ml condensed milk (Milkmaid) and mix very well. If you don't want to use Milkmaid, you can add sugar instead, but Milkmaid greatly enhances the taste.
  3. 3.Once mixed well, refrigerate until chilled.
  4. 4.Extract the pulp from 2 kg ripe custard apples. Place a strainer over a bowl, remove the outer skin, and use a spoon to press and separate the seeds from the pulp. This step takes a little effort.
  5. 5.Add the custard apple pulp to the chilled cream-Milkmaid mixture. Mix well.
  6. 6.Refrigerate for 1 hour. Serve chilled, garnished with dry fruits if desired.

Tips

  • *Use ripe custard apples for the best flavour and easy pulp extraction
  • *You can substitute Milkmaid with sugar, but Milkmaid gives a much better taste
  • *You can use homemade cream (malai) or store-bought
  • *Chill everything well before serving for the best experience
