Pumpkin Kheer

A creamy and special pumpkin kheer dessert made with round pumpkin, milk, khoya,...

6-8 servings
20 min + 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pumpkin (round gourd/gol kaddu)
  • 1 litre milk
  • Desi ghee - 1 tablespoon
  • Cashews and almonds
  • Khoya (as per preference)
  • Sugar - 1/2 bowl (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom powder - 1 teaspoon

Directions

  1. 1.Remove seeds and inner part of the pumpkin, cut into pieces and peel them.
  2. 2.Cut pumpkin into small pieces and boil in a pressure cooker with 1 glass water for 5-6 whistles.
  3. 3.Mash the boiled pumpkin and set aside.
  4. 4.Heat 1 tablespoon desi ghee in a pan, fry cashews and almonds, then grind them coarsely.
  5. 5.In the same ghee, add the pumpkin paste and roast until ghee separates.
  6. 6.Heat 1 litre milk separately and add to the pumpkin.
  7. 7.Mix well, then add khoya (as per preference).
  8. 8.Add 1/2 bowl sugar (adjust to taste) and cardamom powder.
  9. 9.Add the ground cashew-almond powder.
  10. 10.Let it come to a boil and cook on low flame for 5 minutes.

Tips

  • *You can serve it hot or chill it in the fridge before serving
  • *Add khoya according to your preference
  • *Adjust sugar to your taste
