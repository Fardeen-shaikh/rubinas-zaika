Fried Bhindi

A quick tiffin recipe of fried bhindi (okra) made with roasted peanut-coconut po...

3-4 servings
10 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 250 grams bhindi (okra), washed
  • Peanuts (mungfali) and dried coconut (sukha nariyal), roasted and ground coarsely
  • 4 green chillies and 15-20 garlic cloves, ground into paste
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin (jeera) - 1 tablespoon
  • Mustard seeds (rai) - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 1 tablespoon + more
  • Fresh coriander and curry leaves (kadi patta), chopped

Directions

  1. 1.Cut bhindi into small pieces.
  2. 2.Heat oil in a kadhai and fry the bhindi lightly to make it crispy, then remove.
  3. 3.In the remaining oil, add cumin and mustard seeds.
  4. 4.Add green chilli and garlic paste, fry briefly.
  5. 5.Add curry leaves and fry, then add dry masalas.
  6. 6.Add the roasted peanut-coconut powder and cook briefly.
  7. 7.Add the fried bhindi back in, mix well.
  8. 8.Add fresh coriander, cover and cook on low flame for 5 minutes.

Tips

  • *Pre-frying the bhindi makes it crispy and prevents sliminess
  • *Roasted peanut and coconut powder adds great flavor
