Roasted Tomato Chutney

Smoky roasted tomato chutney with fire-roasted tomatoes, garlic, and green chill...

6-8 servings
15 min + 25 min

Ingredients

  • 6 medium tomatoes
  • Oil for greasing tomatoes
  • 3 large green chillies (whole)
  • 15-20 garlic cloves
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 onions, finely chopped in chopper
  • Fresh curry leaves
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons (for tempering)
  • Mustard seeds (rai) - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin seeds (jeera) - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 1/2 tablespoon

Directions

  1. 1.Wash tomatoes and grease them with oil. Place on a wire rack over gas flame and roast until the skin turns black and blistered on all sides, about 10-15 minutes.
  2. 2.Let the roasted tomatoes cool, then peel off the charred skin easily. Remove the stem part, chop and mash the tomatoes.
  3. 3.In a dry pan (no oil), roast whole green chillies, garlic cloves, and fresh coriander until charred and fragrant. Let cool.
  4. 4.Grind the roasted garlic, green chillies, and coriander into a coarse paste with a little water.
  5. 5.For tempering, heat 2 tablespoons oil. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  6. 6.Add finely chopped onions and fry until soft. Add fresh curry leaves.
  7. 7.Add turmeric and coriander powder. Then add the roasted garlic-chilli paste and fry for a few seconds.
  8. 8.Add the mashed roasted tomatoes and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  9. 9.Add salt and adjust seasoning. Add red chilli powder if needed, and sugar to balance the tanginess.
  10. 10.Cover and cook for 5 minutes. Turn off the gas and let it rest covered for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • *Greasing tomatoes with oil helps the skin peel off easily after roasting
  • *Dry roast garlic and chillies without oil for authentic smoky flavour
  • *Add sugar to balance the tanginess of tomatoes
  • *Roast tomatoes until the skin is completely black for the best smoky taste
