Seekh Kabab

Juicy and flavorful seekh kabab made with beef keema and a special homemade roas...

6-8 servings
45 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef keema (mince)
  • Whole coriander - 2 tablespoons
  • Whole cumin - 2 tablespoons
  • Shah jeera - 2 tablespoons
  • Cinnamon - a little
  • Black pepper - 10-12
  • Cloves - 5-6
  • Badi elaichi (black cardamom) - 2
  • Chhoti elaichi (green cardamom) - 6-7
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Chaat masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Chilli flakes - 1 tablespoon
  • Fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • Mint leaves, finely chopped
  • Onion, finely chopped (with water squeezed out)
  • 1 lemon juice
  • 4-5 green chillies
  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 1 teaspoon

Directions

  1. 1.Dry roast the whole spices (coriander, cumin, shah jeera, cinnamon, black pepper, cloves, cardamoms) and grind them.
  2. 2.Add the roasted spice powder to the keema.
  3. 3.Add all dry masalas (red chilli, turmeric, salt, chaat masala, chilli flakes).
  4. 4.Crush green chillies, mint, and coriander and add to the keema.
  5. 5.Add finely chopped onion (squeeze out all water), ginger garlic paste, besan, lemon juice, and oil.
  6. 6.Mix everything well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. 7.Wet your hands with water and shape the mixture onto steel sticks to form seekh kababs.
  8. 8.Heat a tawa, add the seekh kababs, and spread a little oil on them.
  9. 9.Shallow fry until cooked through and golden on all sides.

Tips

  • *The roasted whole spice blend is the soul of seekh kabab
  • *Squeeze out all water from onions for proper binding
  • *Adjust green chillies to your taste
  • *Serve with green chutney and tomato sauce
