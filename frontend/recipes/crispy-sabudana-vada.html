Crispy Sabudana Vada

Crispy sabudana vada perfect for Iftar or any snack time. Made with soaked sago,...

4-5 servings
7 hrs + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg sabudana (sago), soaked for 6-7 hours
  • 4 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • Green chilli paste - 2 tablespoons (coarse)
  • Ginger paste - 2 tablespoons (coarse)
  • Cumin seeds (jeera) - 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • Roasted peanuts - 2 tablespoons, coarsely crushed
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil for frying

Directions

  1. 1.Mash the boiled potatoes and add to the soaked sabudana.
  2. 2.Add coarse green chilli paste, coarse ginger paste, cumin seeds, chopped coriander, and coarsely crushed peanuts.
  3. 3.Add salt to taste and mix everything together well.
  4. 4.Grease hands with oil, take a portion of the mixture and shape into flat round vadas. Make them as big or small as you like.
  5. 5.Heat oil for frying. Once hot, reduce flame to medium-low.
  6. 6.Add vadas one by one and fry until golden and crispy on both sides.
  7. 7.Drain on tissue paper. Serve with yogurt and peanut chutney.

Tips

  • *Soak sabudana for 6-7 hours for the best texture
  • *Use coarsely ground spices for better flavour
  • *Peanuts add a wonderful crunch and flavour
  • *Fry on medium-low flame for even cooking
