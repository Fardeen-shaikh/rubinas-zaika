Kabab Pav

Spicy and tasty kabab pav made with ground mutton, served with a fresh green chu...

6-8 servings
30 min + 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg mutton, ground
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Chilli flakes - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Green chilli paste - 1 tablespoon
  • 2 onions, finely chopped (water squeezed out)
  • Fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • 2 pav bread (soaked in water)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 lemon juice
  • Oil for shallow frying
  • For green chutney: coriander and mint
  • For green chutney: 6-7 garlic cloves
  • For green chutney: green chilli paste - 1 teaspoon
  • For green chutney: salt - 1/2 tablespoon
  • For green chutney: sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • For green chutney: yogurt - 1 teaspoon
  • For green chutney: mayonnaise - 1 teaspoon

Directions

  1. 1.Add all dry masalas to the ground mutton.
  2. 2.Add ginger garlic paste, chilli flakes, green chilli paste.
  3. 3.Add finely chopped onions (with water squeezed out) and coriander.
  4. 4.Add soaked pav bread, egg, and lemon juice. Mix everything well.
  5. 5.Shape into kababs and refrigerate for 10 minutes to set.
  6. 6.For the green chutney, blend coriander, mint, garlic, green chilli paste, salt, sugar, yogurt, and mayonnaise into a paste.
  7. 7.Heat oil in a pan, add kababs with some distance between them.
  8. 8.Fry for 5 minutes on one side, then flip. Add oil on top and fry the other side.
  9. 9.Serve with green chutney and pav.

Tips

  • *Make sure to squeeze out all water from onions, otherwise the kababs won't bind properly
  • *Soaked pav helps with binding
