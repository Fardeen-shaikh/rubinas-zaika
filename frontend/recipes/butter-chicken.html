Butter Chicken

Restaurant style creamy butter chicken made at home. Rich, creamy tomato-based g...

5-6 servings
15 min + 30 min

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 medium onions, sliced and fried
  • 10 cashews (kaju)
  • 10 almonds (badam)
  • Fresh coriander (hara dhania)
  • 3 green chillies
  • Curry leaves (kadi patta)
  • 1 lemon
  • 4 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Dry spices (salt, turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala)
  • Cream
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

Directions

  1. 1.Blend fried onions, cashews, almonds, coriander, green chillies, and curry leaves into a smooth paste.
  2. 2.Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  3. 3.Add the tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. 4.Add all dry spices — salt, turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. 5.Add the onion-cashew paste and mix well. Cook until the masala comes together.
  6. 6.Add the chicken pieces and mix well with the gravy. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.
  7. 7.Add cream, crushed kasuri methi, and lemon juice. Stir gently and cook for 2-3 more minutes.
  8. 8.Garnish with butter, cream, and fresh coriander. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Tips

  • *Cashew and almond paste gives the rich, creamy texture
  • *Fried onions add sweetness to the gravy
  • *Add kasuri methi at the end for best flavour
  • *Adjust cream quantity to your preference
