Mutton Keema Masala

Simple and flavourful mutton keema masala with aromatic spices and a squeeze of ...

3-4 servings
10 min + 20 min

Ingredients

  • 250g mutton keema (mince)
  • 2 large onions
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Green chilli paste
  • Turmeric - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chilli powder
  • Coriander powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon

Directions

  1. 1.Heat oil in a kadhai. Add sliced onions and fry lightly.
  2. 2.Add ginger garlic paste and fry for a minute on low flame.
  3. 3.Add turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder, and salt. Fry briefly.
  4. 4.Add finely chopped tomato, mix well, cover and cook for 2 minutes until tomatoes soften.
  5. 5.Add the keema, mix well, and cook for a few minutes until it changes colour.
  6. 6.Add a little water, mix, and cook covered for a few more minutes until the keema is completely cooked through.
  7. 7.Add chopped coriander, mint, and garam masala. Mix well.
  8. 8.Squeeze half a lemon juice for extra flavour. Mix and cook covered for 2 more minutes. Turn off gas and let it rest.

Tips

  • *Lemon juice enhances the flavour of keema
  • *Add water in small amounts to control the gravy consistency
  • *Let it rest after cooking for flavours to develop
