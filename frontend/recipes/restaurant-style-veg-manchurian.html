Restaurant Style Veg Manchurian

Restaurant-style veg Manchurian with crispy vegetable balls tossed in a flavorfu...

4-5 servings
20 min + 20 min

Ingredients

  • Cabbage, grated
  • Capsicum (shimla mirch), grated
  • Onion, grated
  • Beans, grated
  • Carrot, grated
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Tomato ketchup - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Soy sauce - 1 tablespoon
  • Cornflour - 1 tablespoon
  • Maida (all-purpose flour) - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil for frying
  • For sauce: oil - 1 tablespoon
  • For sauce: onion, thick sliced
  • For sauce: ginger garlic paste - 1/2 tablespoon
  • For sauce: capsicum, large pieces
  • For sauce: spring onion
  • For sauce: tomato ketchup
  • For sauce: black pepper - 1/2 tablespoon
  • For sauce: cornflour slurry
  • For sauce: food color
  • For sauce: salt - 1/2 tablespoon

Directions

  1. 1.Grate all vegetables and add to a bowl.
  2. 2.Add ginger garlic paste, salt, red chilli powder, black pepper, tomato ketchup, red chilli sauce, soy sauce, cornflour, and maida. Mix to form a thick batter.
  3. 3.Deep fry the Manchurian balls until golden and set aside.
  4. 4.For the sauce, heat oil on high flame, add thick-sliced onion and ginger garlic paste. Stir fry.
  5. 5.Add capsicum pieces and spring onion, stir fry on high flame.
  6. 6.Add tomato ketchup, black pepper, cornflour slurry, food color, and salt.
  7. 7.Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and mix well.

Tips

  • *Keep the flame high while making the sauce for proper stir-frying
  • *The cornflour slurry gives the sauce its glossy consistency
