Hotel Style Masala Papad

Restaurant-style masala papad topped with a special spice mix, fresh vegetables,...

4-5 servings
10 min + 5 min

Ingredients

  • Large papads
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Cucumber (kakdi), finely chopped
  • Tomato, finely chopped
  • Green chillies, finely chopped
  • Cumin (jeera) - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Chaat masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Black salt (kala namak) - 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon juice
  • Fine sev (for topping)
  • Oil for frying

Directions

  1. 1.Finely chop onion, coriander, cucumber, tomato, and green chillies.
  2. 2.Prepare the special masala by mixing cumin, red chilli powder, chaat masala, and black salt.
  3. 3.Deep fry the papads in hot oil until crispy.
  4. 4.Sprinkle the special masala on the fried papad.
  5. 5.Top with chopped onion, tomato, cucumber, and coriander.
  6. 6.Add more masala on top, squeeze lemon juice.
  7. 7.Add chopped green chillies and top with fine sev.

Tips

  • *The special masala mix is essential for the restaurant taste
  • *You can use small papads too, but restaurants use large ones
