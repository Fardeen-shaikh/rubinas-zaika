Dahi Vada

Soft and fluffy dahi vada (dahi bhalla chaat) with sweet yogurt, green chutney, ...

4-6 servings
6 hrs + 20 min

Ingredients

  • 250g urad dal, soaked for 6 hours
  • Ginger - a small piece
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Warm water with 1/2 teaspoon salt (for soaking vadas)
  • Sweet yogurt (dahi with sugar mixed)
  • For green chutney: coriander, 3 green chillies, ginger, black salt, salt
  • Sweet tamarind chutney (jaggery and tamarind based)
  • Cumin powder
  • Black pepper powder
  • Chaat masala
  • Red chilli powder
  • Sev for garnish

Directions

  1. 1.Grind soaked urad dal with ginger and a little water into a smooth thick batter.
  2. 2.Add salt and whip the batter by hand in one direction until it becomes fluffy and airy (test: a small amount should float in water).
  3. 3.Heat oil for deep frying. Drop small portions into oil, keeping some distance. Fry on low-medium flame until golden on both sides.
  4. 4.Soak the fried vadas in warm salted water for 5-7 minutes until they become soft and puffy.
  5. 5.Make green chutney: grind coriander, green chillies, ginger, black salt, and salt with a little water.
  6. 6.Gently squeeze out water from vadas and place on serving plate.
  7. 7.Pour generous sweetened yogurt over the vadas covering them completely.
  8. 8.Drizzle tamarind chutney and green chutney on top. Sprinkle cumin powder, black pepper, chaat masala, red chilli powder, and sev.

Tips

  • *Whip batter by hand - spoon won't give the right consistency
  • *Fry on low flame so vadas cook inside without burning outside
  • *Soak vadas in warm (not hot) salted water for softness
  • *More yogurt means better taste
