Creamy Green Chicken

A quick and delicious creamy green chicken curry made with a fresh coriander-min...

4-5 servings
15 min + 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, washed
  • 1 large bunch fresh coriander
  • A little fresh mint
  • 10-12 green chillies
  • A little water (for green paste)
  • 3 medium onions, fried
  • 15-20 cashews
  • A little milk (for cashew-onion paste)
  • 1 bowl fresh yogurt
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder - 1 tablespoon
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon oil (for finishing)
  • 1-2 teaspoons fresh cream (for finishing)

Directions

  1. 1.Blend coriander, mint, and 10-12 green chillies with a little water to make a green paste.
  2. 2.Fry 3 medium onions. Blend the fried onions with 15-20 cashews and a little milk to make a smooth paste.
  3. 3.Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a pan. Add 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste and fry for a few seconds.
  4. 4.Add all dry spices (salt, turmeric, coriander powder, black pepper, cumin powder) and fry in oil for a few seconds until fragrant.
  5. 5.Add 1 kg washed chicken on low flame. Mix well, cover, and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. 6.Add the bowl of fresh yogurt, mix well, cover, and cook for 5 minutes until yogurt releases its water and oil separates.
  7. 7.Add the green paste (coriander-mint-chilli), mix well.
  8. 8.Add the cashew-onion paste and a tiny bit of water to rinse the bowls. Mix well.
  9. 9.Cover and cook on low flame for 5 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.
  10. 10.Finish with 1 tablespoon oil and 1-2 teaspoons fresh cream. Mix and turn off the flame.

Tips

  • *Total cooking time is only 15-20 minutes
  • *Use fresh yogurt for the best result
  • *Fry the onions before blending for a richer gravy
  • *Adding cream at the end enhances the richness
