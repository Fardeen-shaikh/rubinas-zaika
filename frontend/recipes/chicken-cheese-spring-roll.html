Chicken Cheese Spring Roll

Crispy chicken cheese spring rolls perfect for Iftar. Filled with spiced chicken...

8-10 servings
25 min + 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg chicken, small pieces
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • Capsicum, cabbage, fresh coriander - chopped
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Chilli flakes - 1 tablespoon
  • Oregano - 1 tablespoon
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Vinegar - 2 tablespoons
  • Dark soy sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Red chilli sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Mayonnaise - 1 bowl
  • Mozzarella cheese (as per preference)
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Spring roll sheets
  • Maida slurry (for sealing)
  • Oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. 1.Heat oil, fry onions until soft, add ginger garlic paste and fry.
  2. 2.Add chicken pieces and cook covered for 5 minutes.
  3. 3.Add dry masalas, vinegar, dark soy sauce, and red chilli sauce. Cook until chicken is tender.
  4. 4.Add mayonnaise and mozzarella cheese, mix well.
  5. 5.Add chopped vegetables (capsicum, cabbage, coriander), stir fry for 2-3 minutes. Cool the mixture.
  6. 6.Place filling on a spring roll sheet, fold and seal with maida slurry.
  7. 7.Heat oil until hot, then reduce flame to medium-low. Deep fry spring rolls until golden and crispy.

Tips

  • *You can use cream instead of mayonnaise
  • *Add cheese according to your preference
  • *Don't overcook the vegetables - just a brief stir fry
  • *Fry on medium-low flame for even cooking
